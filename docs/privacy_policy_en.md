## 2) Data we process

### 2.1 Content you enter in the App

The App may store on your device:

- tasks (text, completion status)
- calendar events (title, date/time, notes, reminder settings)
- financial data in the “Funds” section (amounts/percents/categories, saved budgets)
- app settings (e.g., theme)

### 2.2 Diagnostic / technical data (optional)

If you enable diagnostic reporting (with your consent), the App may send:

- crash reports (stack traces)
- device/OS info (version, model) needed to troubleshoot issues

We do **not** intend to send the contents of your tasks/notes/financial entries in diagnostic reports.

## 3) Purposes

We process data to:

- provide App functionality (tasks, calendar, finance tools)
- improve stability and fix bugs (only if diagnostics are enabled)

## 4) Where data is stored

By default, data is stored **locally on your device**.

If we add cloud sync/accounts in the future, we will update this Policy to describe:

- what data is sent to servers
- where servers/providers are located
- retention and security measures

## 5) Sharing with third parties

By default, the App **does not share** your data with third parties.

If we add third-party services (analytics, crash reporting, ads), it will be:

- clearly disclosed in settings
- enabled **only with your consent**
- reflected in this Policy (including vendor list and links)

## 6) Permissions

The App may request permissions required for features (e.g., notifications).

You can manage permissions in your OS settings.

## 7) Retention

Local data is stored until you delete it or use “Reset all data”.

Diagnostic data (if enabled) may be retained by the provider for a limited period (often up to 90 days).

## 8) Security

We apply reasonable measures to protect your data, including:

- data minimization
- avoiding sensitive logging
- secure local storage (platform keystore/keychain where applicable)

## 9) Your choices and rights

The App may provide:

- deletion of individual items (tasks/events/budgets)
- **full data reset** (remove all local data)
- data export (if available)

## 10) Children

The App is not intended specifically for children. If you believe a child provided data, contact us using the details above.

## 11) Changes

We may update this Policy. The updated version will be published with a new effective date.
